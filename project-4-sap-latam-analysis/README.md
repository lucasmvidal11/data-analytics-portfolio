## Project 4 — SAP in Latin America: Business & Market Analysis

### Why This Project
SAP is headquartered 30 minutes from where I study in Karlsruhe. 
Before applying to work there, I wanted to understand their business 
from the inside — especially in Latin America, the region I know 
best as an Argentine from Santa Fe.

This analysis covers SAP's global financial performance, its 
competitive position against Oracle, Salesforce and Microsoft, 
and its market presence across key Latin American countries 
including Argentina and Brazil.

### Methodology
- Stock data: Yahoo Finance via yfinance (5-year period)
- Revenue data: SAP Annual Reports 2019–2023 (publicly available)
- LATAM market data: SAP.com/latam, IDC Market Reports estimates
- All financial figures in original reporting currency (€ for SAP 
  revenue, $ for stock comparisons)

### Key Findings
- **Cloud transformation:** SAP's cloud revenue grew from 25% to 
  44% of total revenue between 2019 and 2023 — a fundamental 
  shift in business model
- **LATAM leadership:** SAP holds 35%+ ERP market share in 
  Argentina and Brazil — strongest position in the region
- **Argentina:** ~2,100 SAP customers with 31% cloud adoption — 
  significant growth potential vs Brazil (45%)
